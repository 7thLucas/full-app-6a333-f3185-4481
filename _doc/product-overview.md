# uDesign — Product Overview

## What It Is
uDesign is an interactive room design tool with a built-in AI interior designer. Users define a room's dimensions on a canvas, place furniture and fixtures at custom sizes, and receive AI-generated suggestions for optimal layout — all in one browser-based app.

## Problem
Arranging furniture is guesswork. People buy pieces that don't fit, spend hours rearranging, or hire expensive designers for what should be a simple spatial decision. There's no fast, visual, intelligent way to plan a room before committing to it.

## Solution
A drag-and-drop room canvas where you set the room size, place items from a curated inventory (chair, table, vase, bed, lamp, door, window), resize them freely, and ask an AI interior designer bot to suggest the best placement for your specific room and style.

## Target Users
- Homeowners redecorating or moving in
- Renters maximising limited space
- Interior design students and enthusiasts experimenting with layouts

## Core Features

### 1. Room Canvas
- Set room dimensions (width × length) via a size picker
- Visual grid-based canvas that scales to the selected room area
- Clear visual representation of walls, floor space

### 2. Inventory Library
Items available to place, each resizable on the canvas:
- **Furniture**: Chair, Table, Bed
- **Décor**: Vase, Lamp
- **Structural**: Door, Window (placed on walls/edges)

### 3. Drag-and-Place
- Pick any item from the library, drag onto canvas
- Resize handles to adjust item dimensions
- Rotate items for orientation flexibility
- Snap-to-grid for clean placement

### 4. AI Interior Designer Bot
- Conversational bot embedded in the UI
- User describes their room use-case (e.g., "cozy bedroom for one", "home office with natural light")
- Bot suggests: which items to use, where to place them, spacing guidelines, and style rationale
- Suggestions update dynamically as the user modifies the canvas

## Brand & Tone
- **Name**: uDesign
- **Palette**: Deep indigo (primary), warm white (background), gold/teal accent
- **Tone**: Helpful, creative, approachable — expert without being intimidating
- **Feel**: Modern, minimal, calm — design-forward

## Positioning
uDesign sits between a free floor-plan sketching tool and a full interior design service. It's fast enough for a 10-minute session, smart enough to feel like having a designer in the room.

## Scope (MVP)
- Single-room canvas (no multi-room floor plans)
- Fixed item library (no custom uploads in v1)
- AI bot powered by language model (text suggestions, not 3D renders)
- No account/auth required to design; saving requires sign-in (optional in v1)

## North Star Operation
**Room design session completed** — a user sets room dimensions, places at least one item, and either saves the layout or receives an AI suggestion. This is the verified operation the product lives by.
