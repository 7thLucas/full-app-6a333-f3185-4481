The Case for uDesign
The Problem

Furniture arrangement is expensive guesswork

  • People buy furniture that doesn't fit — wrong size, wrong flow, wrong feel.

  • Hours spent physically moving heavy items to test layouts that fail.

  • Hiring a designer costs $500–$5,000 for what should be a simple decision.

  • Existing floor-plan tools are technical, slow, or require a subscription.

The Solution

Visual design canvas + an AI designer in your pocket

  • Set exact room dimensions — the canvas scales instantly to match.

  • Drag and resize furniture, doors, and windows from a curated library.

  • Ask the AI interior designer bot — get placement suggestions, spacing advice, and style rationale instantly.

  • Design in minutes, decide with confidence — no signup required.

"Between a free sketch tool and a $5k designer — the smart middle ground."
